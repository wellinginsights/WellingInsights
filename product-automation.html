Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ)

What is included in the base $\$249.00$ price?

The base price covers the **Discovery Call** and the creation of the **Automation Blueprint Document**. This gives you a complete plan to solve your problem, which you can implement yourself or hire us to complete.

Why might I be charged an extra fee?

The base price covers the analysis and blueprint. If during the discovery, we find the work is more complex or requires hands-on implementation (writing the actual script, setting up Zapier), we will charge $\$50/\text{hour}$ for that extra scope, but **only after you approve the final quote**.

Does this include the cost of Zapier/Make accounts?

No. The Blueprint only covers the strategy and design of the workflow. You are responsible for the subscription costs of any third-party tools (like Zapier, Make, or GitHub).
